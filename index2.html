Houston Five-O

Tracking Houston crime in real time and historicaly

What are we doing and why?

We are making crime maps of the city so that Houstonians are aware of what's happening in their surrounding neighborhoods. Houston is the 4th largest city in the U.S., and it has an incredible night life, food scene, and diversity. However, with its beautiful and grimy charm, it has it's pockets of crime.
